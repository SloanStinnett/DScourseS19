\section{Question 1}\label{question-1}

When looking at the effect that vacinations have on disease the general
equlibrium effects can obscure just how effective the vacine is at
prevent a recepiant from contracting the disease. This is caused by herd
immunity, as the number of vacinated individuals increase the likelyhood
of a non-vacinated individual coming incontect with them decreases and
so even amoungst the unvacinated rates of disease contraction will
decrease.
